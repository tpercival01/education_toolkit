How It Works

Aletheia utilizes a client-side DistilBERT architecture, fine-tuned on over 120,000 samples of human and synthetic text. By analyzing token probability distributions and perplexity, the system identifies patterns characteristic of Transformer-based generation.

Privacy First

Unlike other detectors, Aletheia runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. No data is ever sent to a remote server.
